A relaxing color-blending puzzle experience.

Draw lines starting from the colored circles and cover the whole board!
Try to fill the end points with their matching color by blending colors together and creating new ones!

POLYCHROMIA includes many different options such as adjustable visual effects and colorblindness support in order to make the experience as accessible as possible for everyone.
