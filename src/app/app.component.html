A relaxing color-blending puzzle experience.

Polychromia combines numberlink puzzles with color theory to create a unique mind-bending experience.
Solve by blending colors together in order to paint every square and fill every endpoint

Tranquil sounds and serene visuals will help you enter your flow state and lose yourself into the colors.

Paint

Paint the whole board with colors! Don't leave any squares empty.

Blend

Mix colors together to create new ones by overlapping lines.

Bloom!

Fill the hollow circles with the correct colors and make the flowers inside bloom!

Polychromia offers a variety of color palettes to match your unique aesthetic. Switch up the game's visuals to create the coziest experience tailored to you.

Polychromia is a colorful zen garden.
With its minimal aesthetic, clever use of white space and calming sound design, it aims to invoke feelings of peace and tranquility.

Polychromia's soundtrack features piano improvisations along with chill, lo-fi tracks to help you relax and enter a flow-like state while playing it. Just empty your thoughts and solve puzzles to the music.

Polychromia includes many different options such as adjustable visual effects and colorblindness support in order to make the experience as accessible as possible for everyone.
